## Design Philosophy

Every tool in this portfolio was built around three principles:

1. **Outcomes over outputs** — tools are designed around the decision 
   a leader needs to make, not the data available to feed them
2. **Audience-aware design** — every report separates what the rep 
   needs, what the manager needs, and what the executive needs
3. **AI as a force multiplier** — Claude doesn't replace RevOps 
   judgment, it scales it
